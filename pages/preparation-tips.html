Preparing for a Computer Science Major

Your roadmap to success in Computer Science starts here.

Steps to Get Ready

1. Build a Strong Foundation in Math and Science

Focus on courses like calculus, algebra, and physics. These subjects are fundamental to understanding Computer Science concepts.

2. Learn the Basics of Programming

Start with languages like Python or JavaScript. Online tutorials, coding bootcamps, and high school courses can help you get started.

3. Get Involved in Tech-Related Extracurriculars

Join coding clubs, participate in hackathons, or contribute to open-source projects. These experiences will build your skills and resume.

4. Explore College Programs in Computer Science

Research universities with strong Computer Science programs. Consider factors like faculty expertise, campus resources, and internship opportunities.

Additional Tips

Take Online Courses and Tutorials

Platforms like Coursera, edX, and Khan Academy offer valuable courses that can give you a head start.

Practice Problem-Solving and Critical Thinking

Engage in activities like puzzles, games, and coding challenges to sharpen your analytical skills.

Network with Professionals and Peers

Attend tech meetups, join online forums, and connect with professionals in the field to gain insights and advice.
